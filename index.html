Big Apple Club (BAC) was born out of the spirit of connection and true house music. Founded in 2020 in Hamburg during the pandemic, we've hosted over 30 unforgettable parties — from student dorms and rooftop terraces to outdoor gatherings across Frankfurt and Hamburg.

BAC isn’t a place — it’s a feeling. We exist to bring the right people together in the right energy. More than just a party, we’re a movement. You can find us wherever the vibe is real — and you can even bring us to you. As dedicated hosts, we organize and curate unique events tailored to your space.

Live for nights you can’t remember with people you won’t forget.

To experience one of our parties and get the latest announcements, make sure to follow us on Instagram @bigappleclub. That’s where all events drop first — location, lineup, and vibes.
